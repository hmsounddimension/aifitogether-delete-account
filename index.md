# Delete your AiFiTogether account

**In-app deletion**
1. Open AiFiTogether → **Account** → **Delete account**.
2. Re-authenticate if prompted (email password or Google).
3. Your Firebase Auth account (UID & email) is deleted and you’re signed out.

**What is deleted**
- Firebase Auth account
- AiFiTogether profile data

**What may be retained**
- Aggregated analytics & server logs (≤ 30 days) for security/compliance.

**Can’t access the app?**
Email **support@sounddimension.se** with subject “Delete AiFiTogether account” from your registered email.
